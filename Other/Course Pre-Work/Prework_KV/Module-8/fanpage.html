Welcome to the Internet Fan Page

Summary

The Internet is an R&B band from Los Angeles, California. They have released three studio albums and three extended plays since its formation in late 2011. The band's 2015 album Ego Death was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Urban Contemporary Album.

Members

  • Sydney "Syd" Bennett - Vocals
  • Matt Martians - Keyboards, Vocals
  • Patrick Paige II - Bass Guitar
  • Christopher Smith - Drums
  • Steve Lacy - Guitar
